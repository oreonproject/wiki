# Oreon Council Members

The Oreon Council is responsible for managing the community and FOSS development, as well as providing direction for the Oreon project itself. While the Oreon Project is backed by Oreon HQ, and most OS-level decisions go through them, the Council plays a crucial role in community engagement and open-source development.

## Current Council Members

### Gabriel Paesano - Oreon Project Leader
- **Role**: Current Oreon Project Leader
- **Tenure**: January 2026 - Present

### Ali Moumen - Oreon Community Leader
- **Role**: Current Oreon Community Leader
- **Tenure**: June 2024 - Present
